# Glossary

- candidate_answer: The mediator's current best response to the prompt.
- critique: A participant's structured feedback on the candidate answer.
- consensus: Approval threshold met with no critical objections.
- digest: A summarized view of common points and key conflicts.
- mediator: A model that synthesizes and updates the candidate answer.
- participant: A model that answers and critiques.
- quorum: Minimum number of successful responses to proceed.
- round: A single iteration of answer, critique, and synthesis.
- share_mode: How participants receive peer information (digest or raw).
